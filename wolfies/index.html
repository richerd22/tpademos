Wolfie's All Natural Dog Treats

A sharper, quieter home for a neighborhood dog treat brand that already has the right instinct: keep it simple, keep it real, and make it good enough to earn trust fast.

Made in: South Boston Style: clean, premium, direct Built for: quick trust + easy contact
What this is

One clear place for the brand.

Wolfie's started at home when Jane was making chicken treats for Leyna and wanted something she felt good about giving her own dog.

It stayed close to the neighborhood, grew by word of mouth, and kept the ingredient story simple on purpose.

At a glance

For dogs that like real ingredients and for people who want a site that feels confident, polished, and easy to understand in a few seconds.

Why it works

Trust first. Noise never.

  • Clear business name, clean hierarchy, and no extra clutter.
  • Fast-loading page with no external scripts, images, or app baggage.
  • Designed to feel like the brand owns the space, not like a template owns the brand.
What’s in the mix

Simple ingredients, handled well.

  • 100% all-natural chicken treats
  • pork treats
  • turkey meatballs for dogs that need an alternative to chicken

Mina appears on the packaging, and the brand has kept its neighborhood identity intact.
