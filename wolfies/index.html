Wolfie's All Natural Dog Treats

Handmade in South Boston with simple ingredients and a very local heartbeat — the kind of small-batch dog treat brand people remember because it feels like the neighborhood.

The story

Jane started Wolfie's after buying chicken treats for Leyna and realizing she wanted something cleaner and closer to home. She began making the treats herself, and the brand grew from there. Mina appears on the packaging, and the whole thing still feels rooted in Southie in the best way.

Jane Leyna Mina on the packaging South Boston

What we make

  • 100% all-natural chicken treats
  • Pork treats
  • Turkey meatballs for dogs that can't do chicken

Why people come back

It’s handmade, local, and straightforward. No preservatives. No weird extras. Just a small business making dog treats people feel good about buying.

Where to find us

Wolfie's shows up around South Boston through neighborhood spots like Lambert's and Deja Brew, plus local pop-ups and direct orders.
