Our story

Made for Leyna. Kept going for the dogs that followed.

Jane started Wolfie's after making chicken treats at home for Leyna and wanting something she felt good about giving her own dog. That turned into a small South Boston business built around real ingredients, small-batch making, and a lot of word of mouth.

Mina appears on the packaging, and the treats have stayed close to the neighborhood ever since.

We make:

  • 100% all-natural chicken treats
  • pork treats
  • turkey meatballs for dogs that need an alternative to chicken
